# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: October 1, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Lord Nine Field Boss Timer Discord Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Discord bot service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Data
- **Discord Server Information**: Server IDs, channel IDs, and role IDs for bot functionality
- **User Interaction Data**: Commands used and interaction timestamps
- **Guild Settings**: Notification preferences, warning times, and channel configurations

### 2.2 Game-Related Data
- **Boss Timer Data**: Kill timestamps, spawn times, and timer status
- **Configuration Data**: Server-specific settings and preferences

### 2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect
- Personal messages or chat content
- User personal information (email, phone, real name)
- Data from servers where the Bot is not actively used
- Voice or video data
- Payment or financial information

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Uses
- **Service Functionality**: Providing boss timer notifications and management
- **Customization**: Storing guild-specific settings and preferences
- **Communication**: Sending timer alerts and notifications to designated channels

### 3.2 Technical Uses
- **Performance Monitoring**: Ensuring bot uptime and functionality
- **Error Tracking**: Identifying and fixing technical issues
- **Service Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage Methods
- Data is stored in secure SQLite databases
- Server information is stored locally on our systems
- No cloud storage of personal data

### 4.2 Security Measures
- Access to data is limited to essential bot functionality
- Regular security updates and monitoring
- Data is protected against unauthorized access

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Guild settings are retained while the Bot is in the server
- Timer data is automatically cleaned up after completion
- Inactive server data may be purged after extended periods

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 Third-Party Sharing
- We do NOT sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties
- No advertising or marketing use of collected data
- No data sharing with external services

### 5.2 Legal Disclosure
- Data may be disclosed if required by law or legal process
- Data may be shared to protect our rights or safety
- Disclosure only occurs when legally mandated

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 Access and Control
- **Removal**: Remove the Bot from your server to stop data collection
- **Settings**: Modify bot settings through available commands
- **Transparency**: Request information about what data we store

### 6.2 Data Deletion
- Data is automatically removed when the Bot leaves a server
- Manual data deletion requests can be made through GitHub issues
- We will respond to valid deletion requests within reasonable time

## 7. Children's Privacy

- The Bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age
- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
- Parents should supervise their children's Discord usage

## 8. International Data Transfers

- Data processing occurs within our hosting infrastructure
- We comply with applicable data protection regulations
- International transfers are limited and secured

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

- The Bot does not use cookies or web tracking
- No behavioral tracking or analytics
- Only functional data necessary for service operation

## 10. Changes to Privacy Policy

### 10.1 Updates
- This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically
- Material changes will be announced through appropriate channels
- Continued use implies acceptance of updated policies

### 10.2 Notification
- Users will be notified of significant privacy changes
- Updates will be posted on our GitHub repository

## 11. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or requests:

- **GitHub Repository**: https://github.com/karltperez/L9FBT-DC
- **Issues**: Create an issue on GitHub for privacy concerns
- **Data Requests**: Use GitHub issues for data deletion or access requests

## 12. Compliance

### 12.1 Regulations
- We strive to comply with applicable privacy laws
- GDPR compliance for EU users
- Discord's Developer Terms of Service compliance

### 12.2 Discord Integration
- The Bot operates within Discord's platform and policies
- Discord's own privacy policy applies to their services
- We recommend reviewing Discord's privacy practices

## 13. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach:
- Users will be notified promptly through available channels
- Appropriate authorities will be contacted as required
- Remedial actions will be taken to secure data

## 14. Consent

By using the Lord Nine Field Boss Timer Discord Bot, you consent to:
- Collection and use of data as described in this policy
- Storage of guild and timer information for service functionality
- Processing of commands and interactions for bot operation
